Previous studies have reported divergent responses in YAP1-WWTR1/TAZ activities after autophagy perturbations in different contexts. Recently, we identified that alpha-catenin levels determine whether YAP1-WWTR1/TAZ signaling will be increased or decreased after macroautophagy/autophagy inhibition/induction.
